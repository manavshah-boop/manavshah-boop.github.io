Company Promise

Meridian Adjusting & Appraisals was founded in 2002 to provide the highest quality of independent claims handling. We believe our only asset is our clients and we're only as good as our last assignment. This core belief empowers us to provide the highest quality, most accurate, and most timely claims handling in the industry. Our efforts have enabled us to stand above our competition.
